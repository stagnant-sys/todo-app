User guide

Welcome to toodoo.

toodoo. is a tasklists manager that is designed to be simple but functional.

Create

When clicking the '+ New task' button on the homepage's sidebar, the new task form will open and let you enter the informations about the task.

If the tasklist name already exists, the new task will be added to it. Otherwise, a new tasklist with this name will be created and your new task will be directly added to it.

Edit

When clicking on a tasklist in the sidebar, you will display its details. To edit a field, simply double-click on it.

You can edit any information: name, description, deadline, priority, and you can even choose a color to make your tasklist unique and more visible in the 'Filter by deadline' view!

Organize

In the detailled tasklist view, you can sort all your tasks by deadline or by priority.

You can also check all upcoming tasks by using the 'Filter by deadline' option in the sidebar.
